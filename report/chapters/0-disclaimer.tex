\section*{Declaration Regarding the Use of Generative AI}

In compliance with the guidelines set forth by the
Department of Information Technology for the Spring 2026 semester,
I hereby disclose the use of generative Artificial Intelligence (AI)
in the preparation of this thesis.

Throughout the writing process, I utilized \textbf{Google Gemini} as a
supportive editorial tool. The tool was applied across various sections
of the manuscript to assist in refining the linguistic flow, enhancing
the clarity of arguments, and ensuring a natural academic tone.
Specifically, my original drafts and research notes served as the primary input;
the AI was then prompted to reformulate these texts to improve readability and
structural coherence.

It is important to emphasize that the AI was employed as a linguistic
assistant rather than a source of information or analysis.
All core research, methodology, data collection, and conclusions remain
my original work. I have (to my best effort) systematically reviewed and verified all
AI-augmented output to ensure factual accuracy and technical correctness.
I acknowledge that I am fully responsible for the final content of this
thesis and stand ready to defend the arguments and data presented herein.

\vspace{1em}
\noindent
\textbf{Tool:} Google Gemini \\
\textbf{Scope:} Editorial refinement and linguistic optimization across all chapters. \\
\textbf{Purpose:} Enhancing readability and flow of original research content.
